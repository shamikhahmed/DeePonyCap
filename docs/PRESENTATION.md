---

## Slide 2 — Who it's for

Young collectors and parents who need:

- A **joyful** way to track MLP ponies
- **Photos** and shelf locations — not spreadsheets
- **Child-safe** design — no ads, no accounts, no chat

## Slide 4 — Generation checklist

Unique names owned vs pony database per generation.

*“You have 12 of 48 G4 names — here's what's missing.”*

Gamifies completion without pressure.

---

## Slide 5 — Anniversary banner

Ponies acquired on this calendar day in prior years surface on Stable.

*“Happy 2-year anniversary, Twilight Sparkle!”*

Emotional hook that spreadsheets can't match.

---

## Slide 6 — Design language

- Pink `#FF6B9D` + purple `#C084FC` gradients
- Soft cards, rounded pills, floating stars
- SF Pro Rounded typography
- Confetti moments on milestones

*Sparkly. Not corporate.*

---

## Slide 7 — Modes

- **Default** — child-friendly pink wonderland
- **Collector mode** — deeper purple for long sessions
- **Dark mode** — bedtime cataloguing

One app, three moods.

---

## Slide 8 — Privacy

- localStorage only
- No server, no account
- Offline PWA after first visit
- Photos stay on device

Parents stay in control.

---

## Slide 9 — Tech

| Layer | Choice |
|-------|--------|
| Frontend | Single-file HTML + inline CSS |
| Storage | localStorage |
| PWA | manifest.json + service worker |
| Build | None |
