DeePonyCap — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

DeePonyCap is an offline-first app for tracking My Little Pony collections. Your data stays on your device unless you explicitly export it.

What we collect

  • Nothing. No accounts, analytics, advertising, or third-party trackers.
  • No data is sent to Capricorn Systems or any server when you use the app.

What is stored on your device

  • Pony collection (names, photos, shelves, wishlist, accessories)
  • Optional parent PIN (stored as a hash on device only)
  • Preferences (theme, collector mode, haptics)

Storage uses localStorage and optionally IndexedDB for photos.

Photos & camera

Photos you add are saved locally. Camera or photo library access is requested only when you choose to add a photo.

Network

  • The web app loads static files from GitHub Pages on first visit.
  • After first load, the app works offline via the service worker.
  • No cloud backup unless you export a file yourself.

Children

DeePonyCap is child-friendly. Parents can enable an optional Parent Lock PIN for export, import, and delete. We recommend supervising exports and device sharing.
