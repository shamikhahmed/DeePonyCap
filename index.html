How to Use
🦄 Adding Ponies
Tap "Add Pony" and fill in details like generation, type, rarity, and color. All information is saved automatically to your phone.
🔍 Searching
Use the search box on the collection page to quickly find ponies by name. Filter by generation and type.
⭐ Wishlist
Keep track of ponies you want to collect. Add notes about priority and where to find them.
💾 Backup Data
Go to Settings → Export to download your collection. You can import it on another phone or after resetting.
